Concepts
Ideas drive technology. Sustainable innovation is rarely the other way around. I strictly adhere to a set of development ideologies.
Agile Development
Value-driven design, consultative itteration, intuitive UX, long-term integrated solutions produce the best results.
DRY / JIT
Simple. Know how to apply it to everything you do, and know when you can't.
Scripting
The ability to maximise work not done is dependent on creation of run-once, run-everywhere tools to support development. Templating and add-ons are necessary at all levels of a project.
The Planning / Production Trade-Off
As an engineer I love to write cleaner, faster, and more modular code - as an entrepreneur I understand when quick and dirty is the better solution.
People
The days of the arrogant neckbeard dictionary are over. Modern computer engineers and designers need to appreciate that in the push to humanise the machine, no single role in any organization can exist without the input of everyone else.

My brand of leadership and innovation is a shared, itterative process and is driven by a set of community ideologies.
  • No A#%holes
  • Tribes exist and are prolifferating. Ignore the importance of Personal Branding, Open-Source and User Contribution at your own peril
  • Your customers see the same face as your co-workers
  • Burn-out is a reality but weakly proportional to effort. Meaning and purpose drives teams
  • It is more important for Start-Ups to seek investment from staff than from investors
  • You are a character from The Office - know early on who you are.
  • If your product does not generate wealth for your customers, itterate until it does.
